Getting Started
  1. Select a General Ed category or Department and click Submit Search.
  2. Click on a row in the search results to add it to your schedule.
  3. Remove a course by clicking on the row again or by clicking on the event in your calendar.
  4. Save/Load your schedule by clicking either button in the top toolbar.
  5. Use the List button to list your added courses.
